Nikoo Homes V Specifications

Security

The building features elevators fitted with CCTV cameras to enhance security. These cameras offer comprehensive surveillance from all angles and operate around the clock, ensuring continuous monitoring.

Structure

The construction of the building will incorporate a robust reinforced concrete frame to ensure earthquake resistance. As required, walls will be constructed using cement blocks. For various areas within the building, vitrified tiles will be employed for flooring, including the foyer, living spaces, kitchen, dining areas, corridors, decks, and bedrooms. In the bathrooms, ceramic anti-slip tiles will be used to provide safety and functionality.

Sanitation and Plumbing

We provide top-notch CP fixtures and sanitaryware sourced from renowned brands such as Hindware, Jaquar, Parryware, and similar high-quality manufacturers. Our toilets are equipped with wall-mounted cascading EWCs, while other fixtures are floor-mounted. Furthermore, our drainage system incorporates rainwater harvesting, promoting sustainable water management.

Doors

The primary entrance features a Flush Door complete with a frame and all necessary fixtures. As for internal doors, we utilize pre-engineered frames and fully equipped Flush Doors for a seamless and functional interior.
